A Brief History

Lamborghini was officially established in 1963 in Sant'Agata Bolognese. The company's logo featured a bull, a reference to Ferruccio Lamborghini's zodiac sign, Tarus the bull. Lamborghini debuted its first car in 1963, the Lamborghini 350 GTV, a two-seater coupe with a V12 engine. A new supercar technology in those days where there were not many vehicles and people prefer more to go somewhere with sitting in a horse or by walk. He thought supercars will be a very nice way to reach quicker & faster than other vehicles. He named his car name as Lamborghini. Since then lamborghini has been a great success and now a days people dream to have a supercar like lamborghini.
